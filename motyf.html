Branding
Client: Hillary Kenna

This project is about celebrating modernism and its
aesthetically simple approach to design. My inspiration
for the design of the catalogue and exhibition came
from LeCorbusier's consumer-focused 5 points
of
architecture: pilotis,
ribbon windows, free facades,
free plans and rooftop terraces. There was a big
emphasis on keeping buildings off the ground by using
tall pillars, which is communicated through not only
the content of the exhibition, but the layout and
design of the catalogue.
